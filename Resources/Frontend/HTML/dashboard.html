Decentralized Crowdfunding Platform

The goal is to construct a decentralized crowdfunding platform facilitating project creators in acquiring funds from a global pool of investors, bypassing traditional intermediaries. Key features to be incorporated include smart contract-based escrow services, voting mechanisms for project selection, and reputation systems aimed at instilling trust.

Clarification: In traditional crowdfunding platforms, intermediaries often introduce opacity and insecurity. The challenge presented is to devise a decentralized crowdfunding platform utilizing blockchain technology. This platform must ensure transparent fundraising, secure transactions, and equitable distribution of funds. Utilizing smart contracts, participants can introduce functionalities like escrow services, voting mechanisms for project selection, and reputation systems to cultivate trust among stakeholders. Moreover, the solution should adhere to regulatory standards and address issues such as fraud prevention, investor safeguarding, and conflict resolution. Following development, the solution should be deployed on a testnet chain, and both the contract address and code should be publicly accessible on GitHub for transparency and scrutiny, including the frontend component.

Smart Street Lights: A Sustainable Solution

Through the implementation of smart street lights, our data reflects a substantial decrease in energy consumption, fostering a more sustainable and cost-effective urban environment. Furthermore, the system demonstrates a significant reduction in maintenance needs, ensuring prolonged operational efficiency and minimal disruptions in public spaces.
